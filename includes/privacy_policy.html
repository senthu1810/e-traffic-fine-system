At E-Traffic Fine System, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

We may collect information such as your vehicle registration number, driving license ID, contact details, and fine history to process traffic violations efficiently.

Your data is used solely to manage and notify about traffic fines. We do not share your personal information with unauthorized parties.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures using encryption and role-based access control to protect your data against unauthorized access.

4. Cookies

This site may use cookies to improve user experience and keep track of session information.

5. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy. All changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at support@etraffic.com.
